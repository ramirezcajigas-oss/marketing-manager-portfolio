# Marketing Manager Portfolio — Safety & Security (Sample Work)

This repository contains **portfolio-style samples** aligned with a Marketing Manager role in a personal safety / security brand.

## 1) Campaign Execution (Influencer + Content)
**Goal:** Increase awareness and drive qualified traffic to product pages  
**Primary KPIs:** CTR, CAC, CVR, ROAS, Email sign-ups, Affiliate revenue  
**Core message:** Peace of mind + practical protection for everyday life

**Campaign concept:** “Everyday Safety, Real Peace of Mind”
- Channels: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Email
- Content pillars: Education (safety tips), product demos, real-life scenarios, testimonials/UGC
- Weekly rhythm: 3–5 short-form videos + daily stories + 2 community posts

## 2) Influencer Outreach System
**Target creators:** Safety, women’s lifestyle, college life, urban commuting, parenting, travel
**Selection criteria:** Audience fit, engagement rate, content quality, brand alignment

### Outreach DM (Template)
Hi [Name] — I’m Camila, Marketing Manager at [Brand].  
We love your content on [specific topic]. We’re launching a safety-focused campaign and would love to collaborate with you on a short UGC-style video featuring [product].  
Would you be open to details? We can offer [paid rate / affiliate / gifted + commission] and clear creative guidance.

### Follow-up
- D+2: Quick bump + 1 line value proposition
- D+5: Add examples + suggested angles
- D+10: Last touch + keep the door open

## 3) Reporting & Optimization
**Weekly dashboard:** Reach, Engagement rate, CTR, CPC, CAC, CVR, ROAS, Affiliate sales, Email capture rate  
**Iteration rules:**
- Low CTR → test stronger hooks, first 2 seconds, clearer benefit + CTA
- High CTR / low CVR → adjust landing page alignment, offer framing, trust elements
- Strong creator performance → scale with whitelisting / paid amplification
